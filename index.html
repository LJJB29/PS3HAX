PS3 HEN & HFW Installation Guide

Follow this guide to install HFW and HEN on your PS3 and unlock new features!

Step 1: Check Your PS3 Compatibility

Ensure your PS3 model is compatible with HEN. This works on non-CFW models (Super Slim, Slim, and some FAT models).

Step 2: Download Required Files

Download the latest version of PS3HEN and the required HFW (Hybrid Firmware).

Step 3: Prepare USB Drive

Format your USB drive to FAT32 and place the required files (both HFW and PS3HEN) in the root directory of the drive.

Step 4: Install HFW (Hybrid Firmware)

1. Turn off your PS3 and insert the USB drive into one of the front USB ports.

2. Power on the PS3 while holding the power button until you hear two beeps (the second beep means the PS3 is in recovery mode).

3. From the recovery menu, select "System Update" and choose to update via USB. Select the HFW file from your USB drive.

4. Proceed with the installation, and once the update is complete, your PS3 will be running HFW (Hybrid Firmware) 4.87.1.

Step 5: Run HEN Installer

Once HFW is installed, go to your PS3 browser and visit the HEN exploit page, then follow the instructions to install HEN.

Step 6: Enable HEN

After installation, go to the HEN enabler and activate it. Congratulations, your PS3 is now modded with HEN!
