PS3 HEN Installation Guide

Follow this guide to install HEN on your PS3 and unlock new features!

Step 1: Check Your PS3 Compatibility

Ensure your PS3 model is compatible with HEN. This works on non-CFW models (Super Slim, Slim, and some FAT models).

Step 2: Download Required Files

Download the latest version of PS3HEN.

Step 3: Prepare USB Drive

Format your USB to FAT32 and place the required files in the root directory.

Step 4: Run HEN Installer

Go to your PS3 browser and visit the HEN exploit page, then follow the instructions to install.

Step 5: Enable HEN

After installation, go to the HEN enabler and activate it. Enjoy your modded PS3!
